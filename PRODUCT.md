# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Designers and frontend reviewers evaluating DODO TECHNOLOGIES, a Rwanda-focused phone storefront. Customers browse iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, and Google Pixel models, compare simple specs in RWF, and start a WhatsApp chat when they like a phone.

## Product Purpose
Create a frontend design-stage storefront for DODO TECHNOLOGIES: iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, and Google Pixel only; rounded red logo styling inspired by the supplied King-style type reference; generated phone product imagery; product browsing surfaces; realistic UI states; Instagram presence; and WhatsApp enquiry flows. Success means every interested client can send the phone name, simple specs, and RWF listed price to WhatsApp number +250784001835.

## Brand Personality
Bold, direct, friendly, and phone-focused. The interface should feel retail-ready, clear, and easy to contact.

## Anti-references
Do not turn the shop into a general electronics marketplace, dashboard, or backend commerce app. Do not list products outside iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, and Google Pixel.

## Design Principles
- Keep the DODO TECHNOLOGIES brand visible and distinctive.
- Keep every product and category phone-specific: iPhone, Samsung, or Pixel only.
- Make WhatsApp enquiry the clearest action.
- Show prices in RWF.
- Surface Instagram as `nidodos`.
- Preserve accessible contrast, focus states, and touch targets.
- Keep the prototype self-contained and frontend-only.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus, responsive layouts, readable text sizes, and reduced-motion fallbacks.

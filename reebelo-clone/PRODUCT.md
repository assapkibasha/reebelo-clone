# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Mobile-first phone shoppers in Rwanda who want to compare Samsung Galaxy, iPhone, and Google Pixel devices quickly, then contact KG Phones Store through WhatsApp or TikTok.

## Product Purpose
KG Phones Store is a responsive phone storefront with a protected inventory tool. The public site should make it easy to browse phones, understand simple specs, see RWF pricing, and start a WhatsApp conversation with the selected product details. The staff panel lets the shop add phones with Cloudinary-hosted images.

## Brand Personality
Confident, direct, and retail-ready. The brand uses a clear KG Phones Store identity with green retail cues, clean surfaces, and WhatsApp-forward actions.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic marketplace clutter, tiny mobile product tiles, decorative animations that slow shopping, low-contrast text, public admin links, and staff forms that feel cramped on phones.

## Design Principles
1. Mobile conversion first: primary actions must be obvious and thumb-friendly.
2. Show real product context: phone images, names, simple specs, and price must stay visible together.
3. Trust before flourish: clear warranty, condition, contact, and support cues matter more than decoration.
4. Staff speed matters: the admin upload flow should be simple enough to use from a phone.
5. Preserve the KG Phones Store identity: green brand cues, clean surfaces, and WhatsApp-forward actions should remain consistent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA+. Maintain strong contrast, visible focus states, readable mobile type, 44px touch targets where practical, reduced-motion support, and layouts that do not require precision taps.
